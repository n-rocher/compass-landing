Features

Privacy centric

Your data stays secure and private, with all models running directly on your local device to ensure confidentiality and data protection.

One shortcut away

Launch the application with a single keystroke, instantly capturing selected content (files, text, images) on your screen for immediate use.

Ask your files

Easily interact with your files and documents using natural language processing, making information retrieval and manipulation intuitive and efficient.

Multi-language support

Seamlessly communicate in any language of your choice, breaking down barriers for effective communication.

Offline

Enjoy uninterrupted productivity with full feature access even without an internet connection, ensuring you can work anywhere, anytime.

Latest models

Experience the latest large language models (LLMs) at high speed thanks to Ollama, complemented by a user-friendly interface for a smooth experience.

Predictive query suggestions

Receive question suggestions based on your current context and past interactions, enhancing your workflow and saving time.

Work In Progress
